I Still Love Bees

In 2004 we answered a ton of payphones. Two decades later I still think about it regularly, and nostalgia hits hard on anniversaries.

20 years ago, podcasts barely existed as a concept. We also got a ton of high quality audio drama from the game. Why not mash up the old and the new to celebrate?

Starting August 24th, this podcast feed will release weekly chunks of the original I Love Bees audio drama - roughly in line with the anniversary of each original release.

I'd love to hear from other Beekeepers along the way:

Record your own reflections as an audio file, email it to axonsonline@gmail.com, and I'll add it at the beginning of one of the week's audio. You can also email text, images, etc, and I'll add it all to this page.

You must include "ok to publish" in your email. Please include how you want to be referred to.

I stand on the shoulders of many puppet masters & player/archivists [1] [2] [3]. The podcast feed will work through Summer 2025.
